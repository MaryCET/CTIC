Since 1994, Celcotec has been a trusted partner for leading builders, developers, and architects. As a cornerstone of technical excellence in electrical, civil, and mechanical works, Celcotec offers a comprehensive suite of general construction services—ranging from engineering design and consultancy to full-scale contracting.

Our core expertise in electrical engineering covers electrical construction projects, rehabilitation of medium and low voltage, distribution lines, low voltage switchgear, 5MVA outdoor substation, and electrical design.

Celcotec is an accredited member of the Society of Philippine Electrotechnical Constructors and Suppliers, Inc. (SPECS)
