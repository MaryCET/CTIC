Cornerstone of Strength and Reliability since 1994

Since 1994, Celcotec has been a trusted partner for leading builders, developers, and architects. As a cornerstone of technical excellence in electrical, civil, and mechanical works, Celcotec offers a comprehensive suite of general construction services—ranging from engineering design and consultancy to full-scale contracting.
Our core expertise in electrical engineering covers electrical construction projects, rehabilitation of medium and low voltage, distribution lines, low voltage switchgear, 5MVA outdoor substation, electrical design, rewiring and upgrading.
Celcotec is an accredited member of the Society of Philippine Electrotechnical Constructors and Suppliers, Inc. (SPECS).

CLIENTS AND PROJECTS

A Proven Track Record of Excellence

We have a proven track record among some of the country’s biggest institutions, both private and governmental. Our roster of esteemed clients includes Palafox Associates, MOHRI & P.A. Associates, HSG & Partners, Banco de Oro, Citibank, the Development Bank of the Philippines, Great Pacific Life, the Department of Labor and Employment, and the University of Santo Tomas.
Celcotec has also accepted and completed electrical contracts in Dammam, Jubail, Gassim, all in Saudi Arabia.

Electrical Works

Our core expertise in electrical engineering covers electrical construction projects, rehabilitation of medium and low voltage, distribution lines, low voltage switchgear, 5MVA outdoor substation, electrical design, rewiring and upgrading.

Civil Works

We hold a license from the Philippine Contractors Accreditation Board (PCAB) and an Inter-Agency Certificate to handle works on public infrastructure such as roads, railroads, and bridges; Manufacturing plants; low, medium, and high-rise buildings.

General Construction

Expert construction and engineering works involving iron, steel, wood, masonry, and earthworks. We deliver durable structural solutions from the ground up.

Renewable Energy

We provide all kinds of power-generation solutions harnessing solar, solar-wind, and hydroelectric energy. We can design and custom-tailor solutions specific to customer needs, eliminating extraneous installation expenses. We also provide turn-key and onsite solutions.

Telecommunication

We also supply and install voice and data systems and security systems, including closed circuit television system (CCTV), voice and data system, outside and inside plant system, telephone switchboards (PABX and KTS), intercom system, MDF, MMTC and TTC, local area network cabling and distribution system.
